About Us

Ichiban Sushi has been serving the best Japanse food in the Courtenay since 2007. Friendly and locally owned, we take care to see that your visit is pleasant and that the food is savoury to meet your needs. Open for lunch and dinner, dine in and takout. Come and try the number one Sushi restaurant in the Comox Valley!
At Ichiban Sushi, we make sure to serve excellent, well prepared food to our customers. All of the food is made fresh to order and never pre-prepared. All of the fish is wild, not farmed, and all delicous to eat!
